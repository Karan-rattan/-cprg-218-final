A Culinary Journey

From elegant fine dining to casual beachfront bites, our restaurants and bars offer something to tantalize every taste bud and create unforgettable moments.

The Beach House – Fine Dining with Ocean Views

Experience culinary excellence at The Beach House. Enjoy panoramic ocean views while our chefs craft exquisite dishes from the freshest local seafood and premium ingredients.

  • Cuisine: Fine Dining, Seafood, International
  • Ambiance: Elegant, Romantic, Oceanfront
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Reservations: Recommended

The Sand Bar – Relaxed Beachfront Grill

For a laid‑back vibe, head to The Sand Bar. Savor grilled specialties, fresh salads, and sandwiches with your toes in the sand.

  • Cuisine: Casual, Grill, Seafood, American
  • Ambiance: Relaxed, Beachfront, Informal
  • Dress Code: Casual
  • Reservations: Not Required

The Poolside Cafe – Refreshments & Light Bites

Grab salads, wraps, snacks, and tropical drinks without ever leaving the pool deck.

  • Cuisine: Light Bites, Snacks, Beverages
  • Ambiance: Relaxed, Poolside, Informal
  • Dress Code: Casual
  • Reservations: Not Required

The Lobby Lounge – Sophisticated Cocktails

Unwind with handcrafted cocktails, fine wines and small plates in a chic setting—often with live music.

  • Cuisine: Beverages, Light Appetizers
  • Ambiance: Sophisticated, Relaxed, Live Music
  • Dress Code: Smart Casual
  • Reservations: Not Required

Other Dining Options

  • Room Service: 24‑hour in‑room dining menu.
  • Buffet Breakfast: Hot & cold dishes, fresh fruits, pastries.
  • Special Dietary Options: Tell us your allergies or preferences and we’ll customize your meal.
